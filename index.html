Part 1
The website I used for a local

restaurant called bagel gourmet
I chose this website because I frequent the bagel gourmet establishment

Also I was able to identify numerous aspects of the website that could be imfroved to increase usabiliy
List of changes
  • There were a lot of words on the screen that were not needed, or important in
    relaying the information necessary for the learnability of the website, or for the user
    to complete the necessary tasks.
  • The navigation bar and buttons are too small
    and do not stand out to the user.
  • The scale of items and images on the site do not match the size of the screen.
    There is a large amount of empty space that is not used.
  • There is low color contrast in some areas which makes it difficult to read
    words on the screen. This can negatively impact memorabilty for the user if they
    are not able to comprehend some aspects of the site.
  • Accessibility wise there were numerous problems detected that I did agree with.
    Some repeating themes were that the text on the webpage was too small. It was also
    said that there was also a lot of low contrast errors.
